Construction of a Octagon with Compass and Straightedge

Steps:

  1. Draw circle with radius R centered at point O
  2. Draw a diamater, intersecting the circle at points A and E
  3. Create a perpendicular line to AE at point O, intersecting the circle at points C and G.
  4. Bisect angle AOG to create point H on the circle.
  5. Centering compass on point A and setting the compass radius to AH, construct point B on the circle.
  6. Centering compass on point E, construct points D and F.
  7. The points A-H form the octagon.
