Construction of a Hexagon with Compass and Straightedge

Steps:

  1. Draw circle with radius R centered at point O
  2. Center compass at any point P1 on the circle and construct point P2 on the circle at distance R from P1.
  3. Center compass at point P2 and construct point P3 on the circle.
  4. Continue, creating points P4, P5, and P6.
  5. Points P1-P6 define the hexagon.
