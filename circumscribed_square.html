Construction of a Circumscribed Square with Compass and Straightedge

Steps:

  1. Draw circle with radius R centered at point O.
  2. Draw a diamater, intersecting the circle at points A and B.
  3. Create a perpendicular line to AB at point O, intersecting the circle at points C and D.
  4. With compass set to radius R, center compass on points A and C and construct point E.
  5. Center compass on points A and D and construct point F.
  6. Center compass on points B and D and construct point G.
  7. Center compass on points B and C and construct point H.
  8. Points E, F, G, and H form the circumscribed square.
